🚀 Go-To-Market (GTM)

Co-sell, MACC, partner programs, outreach campaigns and everything that drives growth for SDCs on the Microsoft Marketplace.

🎯 Co-Sell & Microsoft Programs
🤝 Co-Sell with Microsoft +20 XP
SDCs can register deals with Microsoft and sell together with Microsoft Account Executives (AEs). This unlocks access to larger enterprise budgets and accelerates deals. Learn how to register a co-sell opportunity.
💡 Microsoft AEs who co-sell with an SDC become active partners in the deal — this can unlock significantly bigger budgets.
💎 MACC & SpendMyMACC +20 XP
MACC (Microsoft Azure Consumption Commitment) is a pre-committed Microsoft budget that enterprise customers can spend on Marketplace software. SpendMyMACC is our tool that helps buyers find and use that budget. Understanding this is 80% of why customers love us.
👥 Partners, CSPs & Crossbeam +15 XP
CSPs (Cloud Solution Providers) are resellers in Microsoft's ecosystem who can sell Marketplace products to their customers. Crossbeam is our account-mapping integration that finds overlapping customers between SDCs and partners.
💌 Outreach Campaigns in a nutshell +15 XP
Built-in email campaigns for reaching Microsoft AEs and target accounts. You won't run them yourself, but SDCs frequently ask why their emails aren't sending. Know the common causes and where to look.
💡 Most "emails not sending" issues are caused by missing verified sender domains or Microsoft AE list limits.
📋 Deal Registration with Microsoft +15 XP
How SDCs can register a deal with Microsoft to indicate intent to close — this gives them priority support from Microsoft AEs and unlocks co-sell incentives.
🎥 CRM Integrations & Tools
🔗 CRM Integrations — HubSpot & Salesforce +15 XP
SDCs can sync Private Offers and Deal Registration with their CRM. Scope is read + write for deals, contacts, and companies. Know the difference between HubSpot and Salesforce integrations.
⚠️ HubSpot has hourly API limits. If an SDC says "sync is slow," rate limits are usually the reason.
⚡ Capchase — get paid now instead of waiting +10 XP
Capchase is a built-in option that lets SDCs get paid upfront instead of waiting for Microsoft's 30-60 day payout cycle. Great for cash-flow-conscious publishers who need liquidity faster.
🎫 Support Ticket Handling
🏟 How to handle a support ticket (the flow) +25 XP
1) Read it twice. 2) Reproduce or ask for a screenshot. 3) Check docs.wetransact.io. 4) Try to solve it. 5) If stuck, escalate with full context. Never escalate empty-handed.
🥇 Golden rule: SDCs rarely remember what you fixed, but always remember how you made them feel.
🚨 Escalation paths — when to call for backup +20 XP
Partner Center outages → Microsoft support. Platform bugs → engineering via Linear. Billing disputes → finance team. Know these paths before you need them.
📘 Where to find technical answers fast +15 XP
Bookmark these in order: docs.wetransact.io (first stop), the in-app help menu (second), internal Slack #support channel (third). Use them in this order every time.
