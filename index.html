DAY4

Finance, Payouts & Integrations

Follow the money — and make friends with the tools.
🏦 How payouts actually work
Microsoft takes a small Marketplace fee (currently 3%, or 1.5% on some private offers). The rest goes to the publisher, typically 30–60 days after a transaction.
📖 Read +15 XP
🧾 Invoicing & reporting
The Finance tab shows revenue, payouts, and downloadable reports. When a customer says "I can't find my invoice," this is where you guide them.
🖱️ Try it +15 XP
⚡ Capchase — cash now, instead of later
A built-in option that lets customers get paid upfront instead of waiting for Microsoft's 30–60 day payout cycle. Great for cash-flow-conscious publishers.
📖 Read +10 XP
🔌 CRM integrations (HubSpot & Salesforce)
Customers can sync Private Offers and Deal Registration with their CRM. Scope is read + write for deals, contacts, and companies.
📖 Read +15 XP
💡 Important: HubSpot has hourly API limits. If a customer says "sync is slow," rate limits are usually the reason.
🧰 Where to find technical answers fast
Bookmark three tabs: docs.wetransact.io (your first stop), the in-app help menu, and the internal Slack #support channel. Use them in that order.
🖱️ Try it +15 XP
DAY5

Go-To-Market, Co-Sell & Putting It All Together

The fun growth stuff, plus how to actually handle tickets.
🎯 Co-Sell with Microsoft
Customers can register deals with Microsoft and sell together. Microsoft AEs (Account Execs) become partners in the deal — this unlocks bigger budgets.
📖 Read +15 XP
💎 MACC & SpendMyMACC
MACC = a customer's big pre-committed Microsoft budget. SpendMyMACC is our tool that helps buyers find and spend that budget on Marketplace software.
📖 Read +15 XP
🔗 Partners, CSPs & Crossbeam
CSPs are resellers in Microsoft's world. Crossbeam is our account-mapping integration that finds overlapping customers between ISVs and partners.
📖 Read +10 XP
✉️ Outreach Campaigns in a nutshell
Built-in email campaigns for reaching Microsoft AEs and target accounts. You don't run them, but customers will ask why their emails aren't sending — now you'll know.
📖 Read +10 XP
🎟️ How to handle a support ticket (the flow)
1) Read it twice. 2) Reproduce or ask for a screenshot. 3) Check docs.wetransact.io. 4) Try to solve it. 5) If stuck, escalate with context. Never escalate empty-handed.
🖱️ Try it +25 XP
💡 Golden rule: customers rarely remember what you fixed, but always remember how you made them feel.
